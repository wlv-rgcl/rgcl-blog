The social side of RGCL


It can’t all be work, work, work. Even when it is work, we’ve always tried to find a way to make it fun. Maybe that’s why RGCL has been going for such a long time!

In this post we remember with huge fondness some of our happiest and silliest moments over the past 25 years. We also care a great deal about each other, and we’ve never been afraid to show it!
